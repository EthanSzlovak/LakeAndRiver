Lake and River Ecology

Welcome to the Lake and River Ecology homepage! We are a company that specializes in the cleaning of algae blooms and biomanipulation of populations that are beneficial to freshwater aquatic life.

About Us

We decided to incorporate after realizing the effects that harmful algae blooms were having on local and distant ecosystems. After researching more, we discovered that there are previously unused methods by which this problem can be dealt with that show significant results when tested on a smaller scale. As we learned more and more about the problems that are affecting our watertables, we realized that there are many other problems affecting marine life, and decided to diversify our company in order to deal with these issues as well.
