About Us

Ethan Szlovak, the co-founder has been saddened by the damaging of many lakes he grew up to love; together with NAME who has an economic and financial background running other companies, they created Lake and River Ecology to aid with restoration of lakes containing harmful algae blooms (HAB). They have spent an adequate amount of time reviewing research literaturs and have found a prominent, scientifically-backed, result-yielding method. The method not only cleans up HAB but also provides a spike in Daphnia Repopulation, providing a slight boost to the fish population, potentially enhnacing the local economy temporarily. NAME has a Chemistry background (both inorganic and organic) which allows for the proper preparation of the method ensuring safety and efficacy is maintained.

Ethan Szlovak

Ethan Szlovak, the co-founder and current Director, is skilled in the technological field, in topics such as software and embedded systems. His responsibility, aside from the general role as a board member, is the proper creation of autonomous systems required for large-scale methods, such as timing of Daphnia release as a function of population into the lake for one example.
