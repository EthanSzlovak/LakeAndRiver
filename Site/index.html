Algae Replenishment

The basis of any biological system is plants, as they can use photosynthesis to add energy to a system that would otherwise stagnate. When toxic algae blooms push out the plants that are needed for life to flourish in a lake, or when the introduction of chemicals renders a body of water sterile, algae reintroduction may become a necessary step to take in order to ensure the continued health of the lake. We offer this service.

Daphnia Repopulation

Daphnia are critical to the continued existance of the ecosystems that we enjoy, in particular the growth of young fish and aquatic creatures, but their numbers aredwindling due to a combination of human caused environmental changes. One of the services we offer is the replenishment of Daphnia in troubled bodies of water, bringing their populations back from a lowered state to their natural numbers. This not only can help clear out toxic algaes from algae blooms, but will also increase the overall health of the biological systems that are supposed to be operating in the body of water, ranging from fish, amphibians, and even waterfowl.

About Us

Lake and River Ecology is a company that is dedicated to the restoration of natural bodies of water through the breeding and reintroduction of organisms that are beneficial to the local environment. We specialize in the restoration of freshwater lakes and the remediation of harmful algae blooms caused by nutrient runoff. We also offer services such as beneficial algae breeding and injection into aquatic systems.
