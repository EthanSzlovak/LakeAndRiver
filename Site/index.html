We offer a variety of cost effective services, from cleaning harmful algae blooms to repopulating freshwater Daphnia and zooplankton that are in a state of population decline in bodies of water, both public and private. Using new, innovative techniques we are able to rejuvenate and revitalize eutrophied bodies of water, and also help non-eutrophic bodies reach the best possible state through a system of breeding, farming, and re-introducing beneficial lifeforms into watertables. The goal of our projects is to bring lakes and rivers back to the natural baselines of zooplankton and phytoplankton that they have deviated from, which is beneficial for local populations, local businesses, and the environment.
