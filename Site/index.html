Lake and River Ecology

Welcome to the Lake and River Ecology homepage! We are a company that specializes in the cleaning of algae blooms and biomass manipulation of populations that are beneficial to freshwater aquatic life.

Our Mission

We decided to incorporate after realizing the effects that harmful algae blooms were having on local and distant ecosystems. After researching more, we discovered that there are previously unused methods by which this problem can be dealt with that show significant results when tested on a smaller scale. As we learned more and more about the problems that are affecting our watertables, we realized that there are many other problems affecting marine life, and decided to diversify our company in order to deal with these issues as well.

Services Include

  • Freshwater Biomass Manipulation
  • Harmful Algae Bloom Removal
  • Positive Freshwater Zooplankton Repopulation
  • Positive Freshwater Phytoplankton Repopulation
  • Freshwater Fishery Zooplankton Injection

About Us

We offer a variety of cost effective services, from cleaning harmful algae blooms to repopulating freshwater Daphnia and zooplankton that are in a state of population decline in bodies of water, both public and private. Using new, innovative techniques we are able to rejuvenate and revitalize eutrophied bodies of water, and also help non-eutrophic bodies reach the best possible state through a system of breeding, farming, and re-introducing beneficial lifeforms into watertables. The goal of our projects is to bring lakes and rivers back to the natural baselines of zooplankton and phytoplankton that they have deviated from, which is beneficial for local populations, local businesses, and the environment.
